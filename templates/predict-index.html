Cat-eyed snake
The Cat-Eyed Snake, also known as the Boiga, belongs to a diverse genus of snakes. Their name stems from their vertical slit pupils, resembling those of cats. These snakes exhibit various appearances, from black with yellow bands to brown or tan with dark mottling. While many species are venomous, their venom is typically mild and poses little danger to humans.
